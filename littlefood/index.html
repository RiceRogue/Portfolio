Little Food Horrors

Platform Unity Completed May 2021 Team Solo Genre 3D Action Cooking

Drag ingredients into a pot and plate your dish before time runs out — while mischievous food-themed monsters steal your ingredients, throw drinks at your camera, and sabotage your kitchen.

Play on Itch.io

Overview

Little Food Horrors is a 3D action cooking game that blends time-pressure cooking mechanics with creature chaos. Players drag ingredients from fridges into a pot, cook the right combinations, and plate dishes — all while three food-themed monsters constantly interfere.

Seven distinct recipes and three antagonist creatures create a frantic, escalating challenge across the game's cooking phases.

My Role

Owned the full project lifecycle as sole developer, designing and implementing every game system from scratch. Translated a frantic multiplayer-inspired concept into a fully solo-playable prototype by building drag-and-drop mechanics, three distinct monster AI behaviors, recipe logic, and a time-pressure progression system that kept player engagement high throughout.

Role — Technical Producer

Gameplay Phases

1. Ingredients

Open fridges to browse available ingredients. On-screen recipe cards display the target dish. Drag the correct items to the pot before monsters intercept them.

2. Cooking

A collision-detection system verifies correct ingredient combinations and triggers the cooking process. Seven distinct recipes each require unique ingredient sets.

3. Plating

Transfer cooked food to the plate before time expires. Monsters remain active through this final phase — the chaos doesn't stop at the finish line.
