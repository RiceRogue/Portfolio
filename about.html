About

I'm Eric Ni — a technical game designer, brand partnerships producer, and educator with interests spanning gaming, anime, film, and fandom culture.

A graduate of NYU's Game Center, I bring both the creative rigor of a designer and the strategic thinking of a producer to everything I build. My experience stretches across the full development lifecycle — from prototyping game mechanics and scripting enemy AI to building production pipelines and forging brand relationships.

My greatest joy comes from building relationships — whether that means collaborating directly with creatives, networking with industry figures, or working through complex problems as a team. I believe the best games are the ones made by people who trust each other.

I operate at the intersection of product and people, helping games find and connect with the audiences they deserve. I'm always open to conversations about partnerships, design challenges, and the future of fandom-driven entertainment.
