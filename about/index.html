About

I believe the best experiences start with a conversation, and I'm never short of one.

As a Gamer Zone Operations Manager at Newegg, I curate visitors' experience and lead event programming for a space where community meets gaming tech. I coordinate events, manage vendor relationships, and make sure every person who walks through, whether that's a first-time gamer, a longtime partner, or a curious passerby, walks away with a new perspective on gaming and tech.

My background in Game Design from NYU Tisch shapes how I think about interaction and community. I'm a technical designer, equally comfortable troubleshooting hardware as I am talking shop about gameplay, mechanics, and design.

Whether it's an industry event or a day of the week that ends in Y,
I'm always happy to chat.
