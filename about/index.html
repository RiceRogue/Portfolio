About

I believe the best experiences start with a conversation — and I'm never short of one.

As a Gamer Zone Operations Manager at Newegg, I design and run the physical space where community meets product. I coordinate events, manage vendor relationships, and make sure every person who walks through — whether that's a first-time visitor, a longtime partner, or a curious college student — leaves with something real.

My background in Game Design from NYU Tisch shaped how I think: every system — a level, a live event, a room layout — needs to feel intentional. I'm a Technical Native, equally comfortable troubleshooting a live game demo as I am networking at an industry event or talking shop with someone who's never touched a controller.

The through-line is always people.
